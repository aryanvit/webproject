Wheat is one of the world's most important cereal grains. It is used to produce flour, which is the key ingredient in bread, pasta, and many other staple foods.

Usage: Besides food production, wheat is used in biofuels, animal feed, and various industrial applications.

Origin: Cultivated for thousands of years, wheat has a rich history and continues to be a key crop in many countries.
